
# 🚴 AdventureWorks Shop — Sales Analytics Dashboard

> An end-to-end Business Intelligence project built with **Power BI** using the AdventureWorks dataset. This dashboard provides actionable insights into sales performance, revenue trends, product analytics, and return rates.

---

## 📌 Project Description

This project demonstrates a complete BI workflow — from raw data to an interactive, executive-ready dashboard. It covers data import, transformation, relational modeling, DAX measure creation, and visual storytelling using Power BI Desktop.

The dataset simulates a retail company (AdventureWorks Shop) selling across three categories: **Bikes**, **Accessories**, and **Clothing**.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Retail businesses often have access to large volumes of sales and operational data but lack a centralized view to monitor performance. Business stakeholders face challenges such as:

- **No single source of truth** for revenue, profit, and order metrics
- **Inability to track return rates** by product or category over time
- **Difficulty identifying top-performing products** across hundreds of SKUs
- **Lack of trend visibility** — whether monthly revenue is growing or declining
- **No early warning system** for underperforming or high-return products

Without a consolidated analytics layer, decision-makers rely on spreadsheets and manual reporting, leading to delayed and often inaccurate business decisions.

---

## ✅ Solution Statement

This Power BI dashboard solves those challenges by providing:

- **Executive KPI cards** — Instant view of ₹24.9M revenue, ₹10.5M profit, 25.2K orders, and a 2.2% return rate
- **Revenue trend line** (Jan 2020 – Jan 2022) with a forecast trendline for forward-looking planning
- **Orders by Category bar chart** — Accessories (17K), Bikes (13.9K), Clothing (7K) at a glance
- **Top 10 Products table** — Ranked by orders, with revenue and return rate highlighted using conditional formatting
- **Monthly delta tracking** — Each KPI card shows month-over-month change (e.g. +3.31% revenue vs. last month)
- **Most Ordered & Most Returned Product Types** — Quick summary for inventory and quality decisions
